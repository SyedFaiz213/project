About SYFA Enterprises

Driven by Excellence, Dedicated to Precision Healthcare

Company Overview

SYFA Enterprises is a premier supplier of high-quality Orthopaedic Implants and Surgical Instruments, serving hospitals, nursing homes, and orthopedic surgeons across Tamil Nadu and the broader region.

Under the leadership of our owner, S Zakir Hussain, we have earned a distinguished reputation for supply chain excellence and product reliability. We supply a comprehensive selection of surgical equipment ranging from mini plates, mandible and orbital reconstructive units, to large fragment locking compression plates, surgical drill bits, drivers, and complete orthopedic instrumentation sets.

Every product we distribute adheres strictly to standard design parameters, material grades (Surgical Grade Stainless Steel 316LVM and Titanium Alloy), and rigorous quality certifications to ensure successful clinical outcomes.

Our Mission

To empower orthopaedic surgeons with the finest grade surgical implants and tools. We aim to ensure clinical success, increase patient safety, and minimize operation wait times through a robust, responsive supply chain network.

Our Vision

To be recognized as the premier and most trusted medical device supply channel in the healthcare sector, defined by precision, transparency, customer-first service, and technological innovation like our automated order portal.
